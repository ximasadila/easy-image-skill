# Product Photo

## Use Cases
- E-commerce main images
- Product detail pages
- Product catalogs
- Social media showcase
- Advertising materials

## Defaults
- Aspect ratio: 1:1 (e-commerce standard)
- Style: Clean/Minimal
- Background: Pure white or gradient

## Core Elements
| Element | Required | Default | Description |
|---------|----------|---------|-------------|
| Product | Yes | - | Product type and features |
| Background | No | White | Background type |
| Angle | No | 45 degrees | Shooting angle |
| Lighting | No | Soft | Lighting effect |
| Style | No | Minimal | Visual style |

## Style Presets

### Clean/Minimal
- Use for: E-commerce main images, official product photos, catalog display
- Features: Pure background, centered product, clear details, professional look
- Template: `Create a professional e-commerce product photo of {product}. Center the product on a pure white background with soft, even studio lighting. Capture sharp details with minimal or no shadows. The image should be ready for online store listings with commercial photography quality.`

### Lifestyle Context
- Use for: Usage scenario display, social media, brand storytelling
- Features: Real setting, in-use state, immersive, lifestyle feel
- Template: `Create a lifestyle product photograph showing {product} being used in {context}. Place the product in a natural, realistic setting that tells a story about how it fits into everyday life. Use {color} ambient tones and natural lighting to create an authentic, aspirational atmosphere that feels relatable.`

### Dramatic/Hero
- Use for: Premium products, tech products, flagship showcase
- Features: Strong lighting contrast, texture emphasis, visual impact, premium feel
- Template: `Create a dramatic hero shot of {product} that emphasizes its premium quality. Use bold lighting with strong highlights and shadows, adding {color} accent lighting to create visual impact. Highlight the textures and materials to convey luxury. The composition should feel cinematic and powerful, suitable for high-end advertising.`

### Flat Lay
- Use for: Fashion accessories, beauty/skincare, stationery sets, gift boxes
- Features: Top-down view, arranged layout, complementary props, aesthetic feel
- Template: `Create a flat lay product photograph of {product} shot from directly above. Arrange the product aesthetically on a {color} surface with carefully chosen complementary props. Balance the composition to create an Instagram-worthy image with lifestyle marketing appeal.`

### Detail Close-up
- Use for: Craftsmanship showcase, material emphasis, feature explanation
- Features: Macro detail, sharp focus, texture highlight, technical precision
- Template: `Create an extreme close-up photograph of {product} focusing on {feature}. Capture the texture and craftsmanship in sharp detail using {color} lighting. Use a shallow depth of field to blur the background and draw attention to the material quality and finish.`

## Completion Rules
- **Product not provided**: Request user to specify - this is required
- **Background not specified**:
  - E-commerce use → Pure white background
  - Social media → Light gradient or scene
  - Premium products → Dark background for texture
- **Angle not specified**:
  - General products → 45-degree three-quarter view
  - Flat products → Top-down
  - Bottles/containers → Front with slight side angle
- **Lighting not specified**:
  - Everyday products → Soft even light
  - Tech products → Dramatic side light
  - Food/beverage → Natural light feel
- **Style not specified**: Default to clean/minimal style
- **Ratio adjustment**: Auto-adjust based on platform (Taobao/JD 1:1, Xiaohongshu 3:4, detail pages 16:9)
